Theological Education by Extension (TEE)

TEE, as an alternative leadership training system, brings theological, biblical and practical leadership training to people right where they live and worship.



Connect with us >

TEE AT PCEA J.G PARISH


TEE programme was launched in Sukari Parish on 31st January 1999 by the then Director of TEE Rev. David Gathanju. At that time, J.G Memorial church was under Kimbo Outreach.

The TEE programmes that have taken place in Sukari Parish and are still continuing includes:

Adopt an Inmate Initiative (Makueni Prison) 05 To adopt 5 more at King’ong’o Prison



In Sukari Parish, we run Theological Education by Extension (TEE) programs such as TEE Diploma, TEE Higher Diploma, Holistic Nurturing of Children and Diploma in Guidance and Counselling. Programs are offered by Extension by Presbyterian University of East Africa and are disseminated at the local church level with an intention of training interested members more about the bible and other relevant contemporary issues.
