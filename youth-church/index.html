Welcome to GWAV Church

Join a fellowship

Gwav is our youth church that came to life in 2008 following a need to form a vibrant fellowship where members from the age of 20 to 35 would connect and build each other concerning God and life.
In our youth fellowship, expect to have the best times of your life as we pursue a depth of God that is treasure.

Our Ministries!

Praise & Worship

Our Praise and worship team is all about using their given talent in music to lead the congregation into worshiping God in truth and in spirit though songs. The aim is to create an atmosphere where our members have a safe space to exalt God freely.

Bible Study

The Word is the food of the spirit. As we continue taking in the Word, we build a strong spirit within us and grow our faith. At Gwav, we constantly look to build our members in the knowledge of the Word and encouraging them to live it out in their daily lives.

Hospitality

As in 1st Peter 4:8 “And above all things have fervent charity among yourselves: for charity shall cover a multitude of sins”, we want every one who comes to GWAV to meet a welcoming atmosphere filled with comfort and the freedom to serve and be served.

Men’s Fellowship

PCYMF is a ministry under the GWAV Church and the PCMF fellowship. Our mission is to grow men to serve the church, community and grow in our core paradigms: God, Family, Service. It stems from Romans:12.11; Never be lacking in zeal, but keep your spiritual favour, serving the Lord.

Ladies Ministry

Ladies fellowship is a ministry under the GWAV Church whose mission is to grow young women to serve the church, community and bring them together for Christian fellowship, worship, and service. We desire that our generation of ladies will grow into God’s design.

Group Fellowships

Knowing that a cord of three strands cannot be broken, we are a community that largley values fellowship. We always meet every week in smaller groups. In our fellowships, our members find a family of young Christians with whom they can grow in life and towards God.

Generational Leadership



“There is no such thing as a self-made spiritual leader. A true leader influences others spiritually only because the Spirit works in and through him to a greater degree than in those he leads.”



J. Oswald Sanders

Youth Coordinator


Here at GWAV our greatest aim is to pass the faith from one generation to the next. In doing this we create lifelong followers of Jesus as we seek to sustain our church as the place for future generations to worship and grow. We do our best to create consistency between the church and the youth ministry experience through both theological and sound biblical teachings which are in line with the larger vision and mission of our church.

We have vibrant programs that run throughout the week. Our Sunday GWAV Service runs from 10am -12 noon and its such wonderful experience open for every like- minded youth. All are welcome as we Grow with a Vision!
