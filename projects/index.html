Sukari Presbyterian Academy



Sukari Presbyterian Academy (SPA) is a Christian based educational institution. It is a flagship development project sponsored by PCEA J.G Memorial Church and modelled as a tool for evangelism. It admits pupils of all denominations with a catchment area around Kwabira, Kiambu County.

Sukari Presbyterian Junior High School



As the change in curriculum continues to shape up, plans are underway to have a Junior High School. This has been brought about by the change from 8-4-4 system to the new CBC where pupils will be transitioning to high school after completion of grade 6.

The junior high school will encompass the years 7, 8 and 9 after completion of the 6 years in primary school. To get to junior high, learners will be undertaking national examinations that will be graded 40% to add to 60% that the learners scored in the grades 4, 5 and 6.
