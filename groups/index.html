Woman’s Guild



We believe that women are an expression of God’s personality; in that when he created them, he created them male and female. Our ladies’ fellowship builds up women into who God fashioned for them. We seek to bring together women and girls for Christian fellowship, worship, and service.

PCMF



In a generation where manhood is in question, we are bent on bringing up a generation of men are a fashion of Jesus Christ. Our men’s fellowship has a clarion call to draw and lead men to Christ. It seeks to transform and equip men to be disciples of Jesus Christ for service to God and humanity, raising a generation of leaders who are agents of change.

Justice Peace & Reconciliation Committee



Believing that we have been entrusted with a ministry not only of the souls of men, but also to the bodies and minds and following the example of its master, who went about doing good, claims the right, as it shall see it fit, to undertake educational, medical or charitable work for the benefit of all who desire it promoting the mental, physical and moral welfare of the whole community.

Boys and Girls Brigade



This uniformed church group is focused on training members in Christian discipline and spiritual, physical, educational and social growth. Their agenda is to reach out to the young people teaching them the meaning of having a living relationship with God the creator, and make them contribute positively towards the community, the church and the world at large.

Young Couples

Guidance & Counselling



PCEA Sukari offers both Pastoral Counselling and Psychosocial Counselling under the Guidance and Counselling Department.

Our Pastoral Counselling has gone a notch higher, from the traditional religious and Spiritual Counselling to pastoral psychotherapy which integrates theology and the behavioral sciences. This is either offered by the Pastoral Team or a Team of professionals under the Guidance and Counselling Department in the Church.
