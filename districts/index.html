Join a fellowship Find a district

We don’t give up the habit of meeting together


We believe that when God created us, he deep seated within us an affinity for relationships and being part of a community. As in Romans 12, we are members of each other and are most effective when we bring our portions of Christ together; connecting with each other towards what God has called us to achieve. Our district fellowships provide a warm environment where you can connect with other members within your area, edifying each other and ministering to the community together. You will find a family who are in your stage in life, and with whom you can walk with through the seasons of live. We would love to have you for our district fellowships!
