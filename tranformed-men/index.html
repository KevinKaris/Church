Transformed Men Series

Transformation means a marked change in form, nature or appearance. This is what we seek to achieve together. Proverbs 27:17 As iron sharpens iron, so one man sharpens another.



Connect with us >

Why Transformed Men?



This transformation journey is about strengthening men to be fit for purpose pillars in the house of God, their homes and society.

Areas of discussion



Topic 1: Man and God – Restoring the kingdom man .

Topic 2: Men in the bible – Fathers of the faith.

Topic 3: Man & Family – The art of Fathering.

Topic 4: Man and Money – Making cents.

Topic 5: Man and Failure – Picking up the pieces.

Topic 6: Man and Health – Be strong and of good courage’’

This series will;



Enable men understand deeply the tenets of PCMF. Equip men to be true spiritual leaders in the Presbyterian church Make men more valuable in Christian ministry. Create opportunities for men to fellowship together. Foster a culture of learning

‘’Christianity is not modifying behavior and becoming more religious. It’s about long-term transformation of the heart and the mind.’’

We have an ongoing registration for the upcoming two classes that will run concurrently; One for the Youth between 19-35 and for the senior men over 35 years.
