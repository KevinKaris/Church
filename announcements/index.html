Announcements

Get the latest on goings-on within the church

Follow on what is happening within the parish


There being a number of activities and events happening every week, ensure that you are up to date with the information that is circulating within the parish. Check out this page every week to see what’s going on around.

Proper Wearing of Masks

All members are requested and reminded to have their face masks properly cover their nose and mouth for the entire duration of the service. Let’s be mindful of the others welfare and that of our families.

INTERNAL AUDIT

There will be Internal Audit for all Groups, Institutions, CBM and Parish on 29th October 2022 from 7am. All are requested to prepare for the same.

FINANCE COMMITTEE MEETING

All Treasurers from Districts and Parish Groups are notified that there will be Finance Committee meeting on 23/10/22 from 7am in the Executive Boardroom.

RETIREMENT SERVICE

We shall have retirement service for Elder Caroline Ngunjiri of Highway District on 30th October 2022

WOMAN’S GUILD

Parish Minister together with Woman’s Guild, Ladies Ministry invites all Ladies to 6th -5th Sunday Ladies Inter-denomination fellowship on Sunday 30th October 2022 from 2:30pm in the Church. Invited Speaker will be servant of God Pastor Salma Kola. Welcome and invite a neighbour.

ROPES 2022B REGISTRATION

The ROPES 2022b registration is on going. Both camps will be in December 2022. For more details contact Wamahiga Wambugu on 0725655355 or Jack Mbuthia on 0729393973.

THEOLOGICAL EDUCATION BY EXTENSION(TEE)

Members who are interested in joining TEE are requested to register with the TEE Coordinator Prof.Elyjoy Micheni via her no.0722790482. The following programmes are on offer.
1. Holistic Nurturing of Children 2. Basic TEE 3. Guidance and Psychological Counselling
